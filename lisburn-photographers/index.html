Lisburn Photography was opened by Jamie and Lisa in 2015. It is located near Bow Street Mall in Lisburn. It has gone from strength to strength and we undertake work all over Northern Ireland. Recently we have been involved as part of a local initative to capture some of the best known landmarks in the Lagan Valley area. This has been a great experience for us to work with the local council, history groups and many others. We are all passionate about what we do and aim to give our clients the best experience possible. Some of the work we do includes the following:

  • Weddings
  • Landscapes
